About Us!

Hello! We are Caitlin, Mohamed, and Stephanie; the creators of Zooquarium. We wanted to create an application where you could search for aquariums and zoos in your area as well as learn about the animals they might have there. Who doesn't love going to the zoo or aquarium and looking at cool animals? All of the aquariums and zoos listed in our app are part of the Association of Zoos and Aquariums. They make sure that the animals are being treated and cared for properly as well as many other aspects.
